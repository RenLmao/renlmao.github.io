Modeled Engine

This is a small assignment in which, in Autodesk Inventor, I have prepared an engine model.
The emphasis was on a workable crankcase design.
I used the software's parametric tools to model the crankcase to accommodate the engine components correctly for proper interaction and motion.
It included essential features such as oil ways and mounting points for the cylinders.
The project helped me in acquiring practical experience in 3D CAD modeling, precision engineering, and fundamentals of engine design.
Though it was a small task, it provided a very great insight into mechanical systems and how the different design principles can be applied to real-life situations.
